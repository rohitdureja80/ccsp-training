# Cloud Risk and Governance

**Domain:** 1 - Cloud Concepts, Architecture & Design  
**Priority:** 🔴 Critical  
**Exam Frequency:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐

## Overview

Cloud governance defines how cloud services are selected, secured, monitored, and controlled. Risk management ensures cloud decisions align with business objectives and acceptable risk levels.

## Governance vs Management

| Concept | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Governance | Are we doing the right things? |
| Management | Are we doing things correctly? |

## Key Terms

### Risk Appetite

The amount and type of risk an organization is willing to accept in pursuit of objectives.

### Risk Tolerance

The acceptable variation around risk appetite.

### Policies

High-level management statements that define expectations.

### Standards

Mandatory requirements that support policies.

### Procedures

Step-by-step instructions for performing specific tasks.

### Guidelines

Recommended practices that are usually not mandatory.

## Cloud Governance Areas

- Identity and access governance
- Data governance
- Cost governance
- Compliance governance
- Security architecture standards
- Vendor and third-party risk management
- Resource tagging and ownership

## CCSP Exam Tips

- Governance is strategic.
- Management is operational.
- Policies say what must be done.
- Procedures explain how to do it.
