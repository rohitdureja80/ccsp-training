## Exam Mindset

For Domain 1 questions, ask:

1. What cloud service model is being used?
2. Who owns the control under shared responsibility?
3. Is the question about architecture, governance, or operations?
4. Is the best answer business-aligned, risk-based, and cloud-appropriate?

## Common Exam Traps

- Assuming the CSP secures everything
- Confusing PaaS with IaaS
- Confusing interoperability with portability
- Treating Zero Trust as a product instead of an architecture
- Forgetting that identity is central in cloud security
