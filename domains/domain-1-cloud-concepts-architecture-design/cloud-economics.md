# Cloud Economics

**Domain:** 1 - Cloud Concepts, Architecture & Design  
**Priority:** 🟡 Important  
**Exam Frequency:** ⭐⭐⭐

## Overview

Cloud computing changes how organizations pay for technology. Instead of purchasing and maintaining large amounts of infrastructure upfront, organizations can consume services on demand.

## CAPEX vs OPEX

| Model | Description |
|---|---|
| CAPEX | Capital expenditure; upfront purchase of assets such as servers and data center equipment. |
| OPEX | Operational expenditure; ongoing consumption-based spending such as monthly cloud usage. |

Traditional data centers often emphasize CAPEX. Cloud often shifts spending toward OPEX.

## Cost Governance Terms

### Chargeback

Business units are directly billed for their cloud usage.

### Showback

Business units are shown their cloud usage and cost, but not directly billed.

### Tagging

Cloud resources are labeled for ownership, cost allocation, and governance.

## Cloud Cost Risks

- Resource sprawl
- Overprovisioning
- Unused resources
- Poor tagging
- Uncontrolled auto scaling
- Lack of budgets or alerts

## CCSP Exam Tips

- Pay-as-you-go is a major cloud economic benefit.
- Elasticity can reduce waste, but poor governance can increase cost.
- Tagging is important for cost visibility and accountability.
